Woven Head Cap

Fortitude Fabrics' Woven Head Cap is engineered for industrial and cleanroom environments where complete hair containment and particle control are critical. Constructed from high-density woven fabric with an ergonomic contoured fit, it provides a secure, comfortable seal around the head — preventing hair and particulate contamination from reaching sensitive production areas across pharmaceutical, food processing, and electronics manufacturing floors.

Precision Particle Control, Shift After Shift

The Fortitude Fabrics Woven Head Cap uses a high-density weave structure that traps hair and particulates at the source, preventing contamination from entering sterile or regulated production zones. Unlike disposable alternatives, the woven construction maintains its particle retention performance across repeated wash and sterilisation cycles — making it a more reliable and cost-effective solution for facilities with strict contamination control protocols.

Manufactured at our facility in Wyra, Khammam, Telangana, each head cap is cut and sewn to ergonomic specifications that distribute pressure evenly across the skull — eliminating the discomfort that comes from poorly fitted caps during long shifts. Available in multiple sizes and configurable with custom colour coding or logo embroidery for multi-department workforce management programmes.
