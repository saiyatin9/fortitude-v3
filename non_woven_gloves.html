Non-Woven Gloves

Fortitude Fabrics' Non-Woven Gloves are engineered for light-duty hand protection in hygiene-critical and contamination-controlled environments. Manufactured from HMHD plastic using advanced heat-sealing technology, they deliver a smooth, puncture-resistant barrier that keeps hands clean and products uncontaminated — ideal for food handling, pharma, laboratory, and industrial applications where single-use hygiene is non-negotiable.

Precision Hand Protection for Hygiene-Sensitive Work

Fortitude Fabrics' Non-Woven Gloves are made from a high-quality HMHD and LLDPE granule blend, processed using heat-sealing technology that creates a clean, consistent seam with no weak points. The result is a glove that resists punctures, tears, and liquid contamination across a broad range of light-duty industrial and hygiene applications.

Manufactured at our facility in Wyra, Khammam, Telangana, these gloves are available in bulk quantities to meet the high-turnover demands of food processing lines, pharmaceutical packaging, laboratory operations, and cleanroom assembly. Their smooth finish ensures dexterity is not compromised, and their latex-free construction makes them safe for users with latex sensitivities.
