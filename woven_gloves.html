Woven Gloves

Fortitude Fabrics' Woven Gloves are precision-engineered for industrial and regulated environments where hand protection must not come at the cost of dexterity. Constructed from high-density woven textile with an ergonomic fit, they deliver reliable protection against abrasion, particulate contamination, and minor chemical exposure — while preserving the tactile sensitivity required for precision assembly, handling, and inspection tasks.

Dexterity and Protection in Equal Measure

The Fortitude Fabrics Woven Gloves are constructed from a high-density textile weave that balances structural integrity with fingertip sensitivity — a combination that is difficult to achieve with thicker rubber or nitrile alternatives. The ergonomic cut follows the natural curvature of the hand, reducing fatigue during repetitive precision tasks such as component placement, quality inspection, and laboratory sample handling.

Each pair is finished to tight dimensional tolerances with reinforced stitching at the thumb crotch and fingertip zones — the highest wear points in industrial glove use. Available in bulk quantities with custom sizing options and colour variants for department-level workforce identification.
