Non-Woven Shoe Cover

Fortitude Fabrics' Non-Woven Shoe Cover is engineered for contamination-sensitive environments where floor hygiene and cross-contamination prevention are critical. Made from premium PP Spunbond non-woven fabric (20-60 GSM), it delivers a lightweight, breathable, and tear-resistant barrier over footwear.

Breathable Protection for Hygiene-Critical Floors

Our non-woven shoe covers offer a superior alternative to plastic PE covers, which often lack breathability and can cause discomfort during long shifts. Constructed from high-quality PP Spunbond, these covers allow air circulation while maintaining a robust barrier against dust, fibers, and liquid splashes.

Manufactured in our state-of-the-art facility in Wyra, Khammam, Telangana, every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure consistent seam strength and elastic durability.
