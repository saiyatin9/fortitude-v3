Floor Safety and Contamination Control, Combined

The Fortitude Fabrics Woven Shoe Cover solves two problems simultaneously: it prevents dirt, dust, and contaminants on the soles of footwear from entering regulated production areas, and it actively reduces slip risk for workers moving across smooth clinical or industrial floors.

The heavy-duty woven construction maintains these protective properties through repeated wash and sterilisation cycles — making it a far more cost-effective long-term solution than disposable alternatives. Each shoe cover is finished with a reinforced anti-skid sole pattern and an elasticated opening that secures over footwear of all sizes.
